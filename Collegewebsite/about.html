About Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, established under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956, is a multi-campus, multi-disciplinary university with a global presence. The Coimbatore campus, situated in Tamil Nadu, is the main and largest campus. It is renowned for its academic excellence, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and world-class faculty. With a commitment to holistic education, the institution emphasizes innovative teaching, research, and fostering an entrepreneurial spirit among students.

Our Mission

The mission of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is to provide students with high-quality education that nurtures both their academic and personal growth. The institution strives to provide transformative learning experiences, promote interdisciplinary research, and create leaders who contribute to the welfare of society. With a focus on innovation and technology, Amrita aims to be a leader in education that addresses the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Vision

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham envisions being a globally recognized institution, known for its cutting-edge research and ability to shape responsible, skilled, and forward-thinking leaders. The university aspires to impact lives positively through education, research, and collaboration with global institutions, thereby creating a more sustainable, equitable, and just society.

Accreditations and Recognitions

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). It is also ranked among the top universities in India and globally. The institution is known for its high standards in education, research, and student welfare, making it a top choice for students seeking quality higher education.

Our Campus

The Coimbatore campus of Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a sprawling 400-acre campus, surrounded by the serene beauty of the Western Ghats. It is equipped with modern classrooms, laboratories, sports facilities, hostels, and recreational spaces, offering students a conducive environment for learning and overall development. The campus also features state-of-the-art research centers, libraries, and an excellent network of support services for students.
