Jobs

Explore career opportunities at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. We offer various job openings across departments, ranging from faculty positions to administrative and technical roles. Join our dynamic team dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation.

If you are passionate about education and research, consider applying for positions that match your qualifications and aspirations. Keep an eye on our job listings for the latest openings!

Manage and maintain lab equipment, assist in experiments, and ensure safety protocols are followed. A diploma or degree in Chemistry is required.
